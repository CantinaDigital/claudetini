Analyze this project and provide a concise assessment.

## What to analyze:

1. **Project Type**: Programming language(s) and framework(s) used
2. **Purpose**: What does this project do? What domain/problem does it solve?
3. **Key Technologies**: Main dependencies, build tools, deployment targets
4. **Current State**:
   - Is this a new/empty project?
   - Is it in active development?
   - Is it mature/production-ready?
5. **Existing Documentation**:
   - Does it have a README?
   - Are there architectural docs?
   - Is there inline documentation/comments?

## Output Format:

Provide a brief, structured summary (5-8 bullet points max):

- **Type**: [Language/Framework]
- **Purpose**: [One sentence describing what it does]
- **Stack**: [Key technologies]
- **State**: [New/In-progress/Mature]
- **Docs**: [Current documentation quality]
- **Notable patterns**: [Any important architectural patterns or conventions observed]

Keep it concise and actionable - this will inform the roadmap and CLAUDE.md generation.
